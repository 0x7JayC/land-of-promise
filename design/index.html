// BUILDERS PROGRAM //

START YOUR OWN
MINECRAFT MINISTRY

12 Modules tailored to turn your team into Digital Gaming Facilitators. Traditional youth work is built on a foundation of presence, mentorship, and creating safe spaces for discovery. Land of Promise doesn’t replace the heart of a youth worker; we provide the digital skin to house it. Over 12 months, we translate the timeless art of pastoral care into the immersive world of Minecraft.

YOU NEED:
  • ▶ A lead facilitator with a passion for Minecraft and discipling the next generations (most important)
  • ▶ 2 other people to help lead and run sessions
  • ▶ A Church or Organisation willing to sponsor you and keep you accountable
  • ▶ A group of children/young people
LEARN MORE
12 MODULES
MODULE 1
THE FOUNDATION
Before we build, we prepare. Mission, theology, the "questions to ask," safe spaces, parental partnerships, and a ministry plan you can pitch to your pastor.
MODULE 2
SKILL BUILDING
Java Server Training: monthly live classes (fixed dates/times) on using games and stories to form fellowship.
MOD 3–5
FOUNDATIONS & SAFETY
The digital space is like the "Wild West." We provide in-depth training on keeping children safe and locking down your Discord servers.
MOD 6–9
BUILDING CONTENT
Minecraft & The Bible: creating lessons that work hand-in-hand with gameplay. Includes 4 Land of Promise Lesson Templates you can adapt.
MOD 10–12
FAMILY EVENTS & CERTIFICATION
Guidance for providing parental support and planning In-Person Minecraft Events. Includes Official Land of Promise Certification on completion.
